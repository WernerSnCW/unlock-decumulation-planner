Critical
1. VCT disposal tax is still contradictory inside the live tax matrix.
Applies to: asset_tax_matrix.assets.vct.on_draw, asset_tax_matrix.assets.vct.cgt, asset_tax_matrix.assets.vct.limits, asset_tax_matrix.assets.vct.on_entry
Problem: You correctly say in before_5_years_CORRECTED that early disposal triggers clawback, not CGT. But elsewhere the same VCT block still says:
cgt: “CGT-free after 5-year hold … CGT at standard rates before 5 years”
limits.cgt_free_hold_period.note: “CGT applies on disposal before 5 years”
early_disposal_mechanics: always uses 30% clawback, even though you have already added the April 2026 change to 20% for new subscriptions
That means the live body still contains a wrong tax rule and a wrong clawback basis for post-6 April 2026 VCT lots. The VCT relief reduction from 30% to 20% from 6 April 2026 is in the government’s November 2025 paper.
Correction:
Remove every remaining statement that implies CGT applies to VCT disposal before 5 years.
Make VCT clawback explicitly subscription-date dependent:
pre-6 April 2026 lot: 30%
on/after 6 April 2026 lot: 20%
2. Pension IHT scoring is not operationally defined for the post-2027 scenario.
Applies to: drawdown_priority_scoring.dimensions.iht_reduction.scoring_logic, scenario toggle interaction
Problem: You define pension_pre_2027: 8, but there is no explicit post-2027 pension IHT score when apply_2027_pension_iht=true. That leaves a gap in the core scoring engine exactly where the scenario should change draw behaviour. Since official policy papers say unused pension funds and most death benefits are intended to come into IHT from 6 April 2027, with death-in-service benefits excluded, this is not a theoretical nicety.
Correction: Add explicit year-sensitive pension scoring, for example:
pre-2027 with toggle on/off: high IHT-protection score
post-2027 with toggle on: pension drops sharply on the IHT dimension
death-in-service excluded if ever modelled later
3. Property mortgage treatment is still inconsistent between the valuation layer and the output/IHT formulas.

Correction: Every IHT formula in the spec must reference estate-value inputs, not total_portfolio_value. Do the same in calculateNoPlanIHT().
